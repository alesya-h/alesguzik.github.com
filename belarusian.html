This Belarusian Keyboard enables you to easily type Belarusian online without installing Belarusian keyboard. You can use your computer keyboard or mouse to type Belarusian letters with this online keyboard.

Pressing Esc on the Belarusian keyboard layout will toggle the mouse input between virtual QWERTY keyboard and virtual Belarusian keyboard. The key will also turn on/off your keyboard input conversion. Pressing Esc on your keyboard has the same function.

Press Shift for additional Belarusian letters that are not visible on the keyboard.

For mobile phones and tablets, touch and hold inside the text area to copy the text. You can then paste the text in any app such as Facebook, Twitter, email, or search app.
